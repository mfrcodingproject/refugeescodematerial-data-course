## Tips for Continuous Improvement

1. **Code every day** - Even 15-30 minutes of practice daily is better than long sessions once a week
2. **Read other people's code** - Look at open-source projects on GitHub
3. **Build projects** - Apply what you learn by building real things
4. **Teach others** - Explaining concepts helps solidify your understanding
5. **Debug without immediately searching** - Try to solve errors on your own first
6. **Keep a coding journal** - Document what you learn and problems you solve

Remember: becoming proficient in Python is a journey, not a destination. Keep practicing, stay curious, and don't be afraid to experiment!
